Spain Tourist Attractions

Sagrada familia

Often considered one of the most beautiful buildings in the world, La Sagrada Familia is a must-see attraction for anyone visiting Barcelona

Royal Palace

The Royal Palace (Palacio Real), is the official residence of the Spanish royal family in Madrid, although now used only for state ceremonies.

Santiago de Compostela Cathedral

Built-in 1075 during the reign of Alfonso VI, the Santiago de Compostela Archcathedral Basilica is one of the most important religious buildings in Spain.

Guggenhim museum

Culture buffs will never get bored in Spain, as the country is awash with fabulous museums covering a wide range of subjects

Plaza de Espana

Surrounded by the verdant grounds of the María Luisa Park, Plaza de España is one of the most majestic squares in all of Spain

Plaza Mayor

Nestled in the heart of the city and built between 1729 and 1755, the plaza is known as one of the most beautiful in Europe thanks to its grandeur and intricacy in decoration.
